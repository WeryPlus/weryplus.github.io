Log In Button!

This button is used for logging into your account!



You will see this window whenever you click on it!



Here you can log into you account using your ID and then click on continue. If you do not have an account yet you can click on sign in.



If you clicked on sign in this will be shown.



Here you can sign and create your account using your ID, name and surnames and then click on continue. If you already have an account you can click on log in.



Whether you logged into your account or created a new one you will reach your account information. Here you will see your accumulated money. From hear you can log out so you can log into other account.




Contained in: Ticket Dialog, Main Window and Prize Dialog.
