Select your prizes!

This is the window where you will choose your prizes if you were lucky enough!

Depending on your game you could see different things right now.



If you got the Star Number inside of one of your prizes you will see this.



Here you will be able to log into your account and receive a 20€ voucher to spend on the mall.



If you got any line prize you will see this.



Here you will be able to obtain 1 or 2 line prizes which you can select by clicking on them as shown.




If you were SUPER LUCKY you may have a BINGO prize and then you will see this.



Here you will be able to obtain a bingo prize which you can select by clicking on it as shown.




After receiving you gifts, if you got any, you will reach this window.



Here you will see what did you choose and then you can finish you game to let the next user use their ticket.



This window contains the following sections:

  • Log In Button
  • Language Button
