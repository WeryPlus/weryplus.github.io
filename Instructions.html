Instructions to the Sitongo Bingo game!

To play this game first you need to insert a valid ticket in the ticket window!



Then you will see the main window.



Here you can see your card with 9 numbers and some buttons. To start you must click the spin button.



Then if the number that came out is in your card you must select it so it is marked. But make sure you are paying attention as if you miss the number you wont be able to mark it later!



After a while you might encounter that you have a line, whick means three numbers are marked in the same line.



Then you just need to click the line button



And if you are lucky enough to obtain a bingo, which means all of the number have been marked.



You just need to click the bingo button.



Then you will reach the prizes window where, if you were lucky enough, you will be able to choose your gifts!
